## Weekly Review Notes

Track these fields every week:

- Which platform produced applications.
- Which UTM source and medium appeared in `/admin/leads`.
- Which page type produced stronger candidates: hub, core, city, or category.
- Whether candidates understood invite-only approval.
- Whether candidates included useful sourcing categories, regions, and experience.
- Whether any post attracted low-quality or confused applicants.

## Compliance Checklist Before Publishing

- No guaranteed job or income claim.
- No fixed salary unless separately approved.
- No public FMS signup wording.
- No direct importer contact wording.
- No request for candidate payment, bank details, or private documents in public posts.
- Use only official `chinapakimporthub.com` URLs.
- Make it clear that application creates an admin-review lead only.
- Make it clear that FMS account access is invite-only after approval.
